About

Written in the Summer of 2017 by Christopher Freeman; Recorded November 1-14 2017 in Dallas, TX by Alex Bhore at Elmwood Sound. This debut album from ADYDA is comprised of long-form single drum takes and simplistic key patterns built and layered into a cacophonous wall of sound. These rhythms and tones are the heartbeat underpinning the immense magnitude of Freeman's vocal arrangements in an attempt to lay the groundwork for a new chapter in creative expression for this seasoned musician. Coupled with Bhore's visionary production and bombastic engineering, the record sits on the edge of Dance, Instrumental, Mantra, and Introspective Futuristic Tribal Maximalism.
